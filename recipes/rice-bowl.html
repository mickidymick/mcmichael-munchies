Classic Lasagna

A delicious layered pasta dish with rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese — perfect for family dinners.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Boil lasagna noodles according to package instructions.

  2. In a pan, brown ground beef and add marinara sauce.

  3. Layer noodles, meat sauce, ricotta, and mozzarella in a baking dish.

  4. Top with Parmesan and bake at 375°F (190°C) for 30-40 minutes.
