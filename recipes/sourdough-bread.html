Instructions

  1. Add warm water and the starter to a bowl. Wisk it until the starter has mixed into the water.

  2. Measure out the bread flour, all purpose flour, and salt and add them all to a bowl. Thouroughly mix them together and add to thw water and starter.

  3. Cover the bowl with a kitchen towel and let the dough rest for 30 minutes.

  4. After the 30 minute rest period, give your dough its first set of stretch and folds.
